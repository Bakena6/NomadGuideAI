# NomadGuide AI — Monetization Strategy

## Core Revenue Streams

### 1. B2B: Regional Governments (Akimats) — Primary

Sell the complete NomadGuide package to Kazakh regional tourism departments.

| Region | Tourist Attractions | Potential |
|--------|-------------------:|:---------:|
| Mangystau (pilot) | 50+ | $10-15K |
| Almaty & Almaty region | 100+ | $15-25K |
| Astana | 50+ | $10-20K |
| Turkestan | 80+ | $15-20K |
| East Kazakhstan | 100+ | $10-15K |
| Atyrau | 30+ | $10-15K |
| **Total (20 regions)** | | **$200-400K** |

**What akimat buys:**
- 20,000 promotional QR codes at airports, train stations, hotels
- Custom branding: "Mangystau Official Audio Guide"
- Priority content (their region first in app discovery)
- Yearly content updates
- Analytics dashboard (top visited places, tourist behaviour)

**Sales pitch:** "NomadGuide AI replaces printed brochures and human guides for 100x less cost. Every tourist gets a personal guide in their pocket, in their language."

### 2. B2C: App Store — Secondary

$7.99 one-time purchase. No subscriptions.

| Metric | Estimate |
|--------|:--------:|
| Annual foreign tourists (non-CIS) | ~1.2M |
| Target: iOS users willing to pay | ~10% = 120,000 |
| Conversion rate (to $7.99 purchase) | 3-5% |
| **Annual App Store revenue** | **$28K - $48K** |

### 3. B2B2C: Hotels & Airlines

Premium package: hotels buy codes for guests as concierge service.

| Partner | Units | Price/Unit | Total |
|---------|:-----:|:----------:|:-----:|
| Hotels in Aktau (20 hotels × 100 codes/yr) | 2,000 | $2 | $4K |
| Air Astana (promo codes for tourists) | 5,000 | $1.50 | $7.5K |
| **Total** | | | **$11.5K** |

## Pricing Summary

| Tier | Customer | Price | What They Get |
|------|----------|:-----:|---------------|
| Free trial | All | $0 | 3 landmarks (demo) |
| Full | Tourist | $7.99 | All landmarks, all languages, offline |
| Regional | Akimat | $10-25K | Custom branding, QR codes, analytics |
| Enterprise | Ministry | $50-100K | All regions, multi-year, white label |

## Usage of Funds

Initial $10-15K from Mangystau pilot covers:
- Apple Developer Account ($99/yr)
- Domain, hosting for website
- Localisation (professional QC for Chinese, Korean)
- Promotional materials
- Reserve for server costs (knowledge base updates)

All AI inference runs on-device — no cloud costs per user.

## Growth Path

```
Year 1: Mangystau pilot + App Store → $30-50K
Year 2: 5-7 regions + Hotel/Airline partnerships → $100-200K
Year 3: 15+ regions + Ministry → $200-400K
Year 4: Central Asia expansion (Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan) → $500K+
```
